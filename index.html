why university tees?

University Tees was founded in 2003 by two students at Miami University, Nick Dadas and Hoe Haddad. They had difficulty trying to organize a t-shirt order and figured there was a better way. Therefore, they started University Tees with one simple vision in mind: to create the best custom apparel experience the collegiate market had ever seen.

what can northwest campus managers do for you?

Our jobs are to make your life easy. We handle all of the order details for our customers! Since we are all students at Northwest, we are available to you for assistance at any time! Also, by using our Bird Bank, we collect the information from your group's order (sizes, quantities, and payment) so you don't have to!
